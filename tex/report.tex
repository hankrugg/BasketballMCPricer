\section{Contract definition}
The contracts we study are basketball prediction market contracts with payoff
\[
X = \mathbf{1}\{P(T) > 0\},
\]
where \(P(T)\) denotes the final point differential, defined as
\[
P(T) = \text{Score}_A(T) - \text{Score}_B(T).
\]
Thus, a ``Yes'' contract on Team A pays \$1 if Team A wins the game and \$0 otherwise.
